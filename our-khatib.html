“Islam is the Quran’s religion and the source of Islamic law. Muslims believe in one God and place great emphasis on His teachings. Islam also teaches that there is a bridge between this life and the next and that through Prayer and fasting, people can make amends for their actions. This includes religious beliefs, how to live a good life, etiquette, and more. There are many ways to learn Islamic knowledge, from reading Quran and Hadith to attending the mosque and learning from Muslim Khatib.”
